Tattoo Policies & FAQ

Please review these policies before submitting a tattoo request. Submitting a request indicates that you have read and agree to these policies.

These policies help keep the booking process clear, fair, and organized for everyone. If you have questions after reviewing them, please reach out before booking.

Deposit Policy

Deposits are required to secure tattoo appointments. Deposits go toward the final cost of your tattoo unless otherwise stated.

  • Deposits are non-refundable.
  • Deposits may be forfeited for no-shows, repeated reschedules, or late cancellations.
  • No appointment is held until the deposit has been received.

Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Please provide as much notice as possible if you need to reschedule.

  • Late cancellations may result in loss of deposit.
  • Repeated rescheduling may require a new deposit.
  • No-shows may not be eligible to rebook.

Touch-Up Policy

Touch-ups are reserved for rare healing inconsistencies and are not part of the standard tattoo process.

  • Complimentary touch-ups are only available within Taylor’s stated touch-up window.
  • The touch-up must be scheduled within that window, not just requested.
  • Touch-ups are for small healed areas only and not for design changes, additions, or reworking the tattoo.
  • Healed photos may be required before approval.

Design Policy

All tattoos are custom and designed by Taylor to best suit her artistic style and your body placement.

  • Taylor specializes in fine line, micro realism, and realism tattoos.
  • Designs may be adjusted for readability, longevity, and flow on the body.
  • Vague requests may delay the booking process.
  • Reference images are used as inspiration, not for exact copying.

Consultation Policy

Some projects may require additional discussion before booking.

  • Clear reference images and placement details help speed up approval.
  • Some concepts may be declined if they are not a good fit for Taylor’s style.

Billing & Pricing

Pricing depends on size, placement, detail, and overall complexity.

  • Quotes are estimates unless otherwise stated.
  • Final pricing may vary depending on the final approved design.
  • Large or complex pieces may require multiple sessions.

FAQ

  • Does submitting a request guarantee an appointment?
    No. Submitting a request does not guarantee booking.
  • Can I request changes to my idea?
    Yes, but changes may affect pricing, timing, and scheduling.
  • Will I see the design in advance?
    Taylor’s process may vary depending on the project.
  • What if I do not get a reply?
    Please check your spam or junk folder and make sure Taylor’s email is saved in your contacts.
